Stock Chart App

Dmitri Slepov

About

This is simple app that plot stock charts.

You can:

  • choose a company symbol, e.g. MSFT, GOOG, FB
  • choose a date range
  • choose a chart type
  • add a moving average

Quantmod

This app uses the quantmod package.

library(quantmod)
getSymbols("FB", from="2014-06-01")
